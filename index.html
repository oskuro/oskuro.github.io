About

I’m a game designer and code tinkerer who love working on games that break the mold somehow. Trying new things or thinking about new solutions for old problems is where I’m happiest.  This is of course best done with other people and perspectives. 

I focus mostly on general game design and making sure the design supports the theme and existing mechanics of the game but I also enjoy level design and narrative design.
